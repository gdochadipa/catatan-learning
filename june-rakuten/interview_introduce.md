## 💡 Quick Tips for Delivery:
1. **Speak Slowly and Clearly:** Take your time. It is much better to speak slowly with clear pronunciation than to speak fast.
2. **Be Confident with Numbers:** When you say "4 years," "4,000 global users," and "20% faster," say them with confidence. Recruiters love hard numbers!
3. **Keep Smiling:** A warm smile makes you look friendly, approachable, and easy to work with—qualities Rakuten highly values.
